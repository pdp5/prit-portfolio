Volleyball

I’m passionate about playing volleyball, particularly during the winter months when I enjoy spending time with friends on the court. Whether it's a friendly match or a competitive game, volleyball brings me joy and allows me to stay active. I also participate in local-level volleyball tournaments, where I can showcase my skills, work as part of a team, and embrace the challenges of competition. This activity not only helps me stay physically fit but also teaches valuable lessons in teamwork, discipline, and perseverance, which I carry with me in all areas of my life.

Music

I have a deep love for music and dance. I enjoy singing and playing the guitar, although I haven’t learned them professionally. Instead, I’ve dedicated countless hours practicing through YouTube tutorials, constantly refining my skills. My love for dancing is equally strong, and I’m fortunate to participate in annual events organized by the temple I regularly attend. These events offer my friends and me the chance to learn new dances and perform in front of a large audience, fostering creativity and teamwork. These activities not only allow me to express myself but also contribute to my personal growth and discipline.
